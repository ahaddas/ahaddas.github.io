01 — About

A little about me

I have a passion for creating immersive experiences that blend storytelling, design, and technology. With decades of experience in image-editing and audio-visual arts, I bring a unique perspective to game development and interactive media. Finally I've added programming to my skillset, and I'm excited to continue growing as a multi-disciplinary creative.

Based in Finland, I am open to remote opportunities and collaborations worldwide. I grew up on the internet and I'm fluent in Finnish, Swedish and English.
